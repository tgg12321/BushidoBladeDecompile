## Evidence

Clears channel flags, calls **two named SPU helpers** (`spu_NotifyChannel` and `spu_ResetCounter`), zero-fills the byte-state area at offsets 0x14..0x21, and resets the 16-entry per-voice-slot state with default volume 0x7F.

The `spu_*` named calls + the per-channel struct manipulation lock this firmly into the audio subsystem.

## Why this name

Resets a single SPU channel (group `a0`, channel `a1` within the group) to a known state: clears flags, notifies the hardware, zero-fills the byte state, and sets default volumes. The 0xB0-byte stride is the per-channel record size.
