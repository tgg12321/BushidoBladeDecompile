## Evidence

**Per-character motion stage parser**: reads a 16-bit value at `g_char_state[a0].field_48` (the per-char 1100-byte struct), scans its 4 nibbles, and for nibbles == 4 or 5 stashes the nibble position and a copy of `field_26C` into a 'pending stage' record at offsets `+0x88`/`+0x8A` and `+0x8E`/`+0x90`.

This may be a different per-char struct or the same struct accessed via different offset alignment.

## Why this name

Scans the 4-nibble 'motion stage' word from the per-character state struct and records the position of any type-4 or type-5 stage nibbles for downstream processing. Part of the motion state-machine.
