- Address: `0x8008D118`
- Proposed name: `g_isqrt_lut`
- Confidence: **high**

  lui $at, %hi(D_8008D118)
  addu $at, $at, idx
  lbu val, %lo(D_8008D118)($at)

src/code6cac.c -- `cpu_check_run_attack`:
  sqrt_val = *(&D_8008D118 + sum_sq);

## Why this name

Per-byte sqrt/isqrt approximation table. The `>>3` after the lookup is the
standard 'normalize back to the original units' shift; the index is a
sum-of-squares (sum_sq) which is the magnitude-squared of a vector. The
callers are distance/range computations in CPU AI and the camera. This is
the inverse-sqrt LUT used throughout the engine for 2D/3D magnitude estimates.
